# Generated Tomb of Horrors

## Legend

Somewhere under a lost and lonely hill lies a labyrinthine crypt. It is filled with terrible traps, strange guardians, and rich treasures—but the true nature of the place is known only to the one who built it.

### Possible locales
- The highest hill on the plains
- An unmapped island in the great lake
- In the wastes of the desert
- At the border of the northern duchy
- Somewhere in the vast swamp
- On an island beyond the realm of the sea barons

### DM notes
This is a thinking person's module. Negotiation requires caution and observation. Read keyed areas as players arrive; never give information characters would have no way of knowing.
